Abdul Jalil Zakaria: Work Highlights

August 22, 2024

My work at GIZ over the past months has been an enriching experience, marked by both learning and applying new skills to impactful projects. I developed a concept note for a capacity needs assessment aimed at evaluating government institutions’ readiness to support the drive of electric mobility in Rwanda. This project is critical in identifying institutional gaps and proposing tailored capacity-building initiatives to advance electric mobility in the country.

I am particularly excited about the upcoming field survey, where I will analyze the findings and engage with various stakeholders in Rwanda’s transport ecosystem, further enhancing my stakeholder engagement skills. These experiences are invaluable to my career development, paving the way for me to become a policy analyst and thought leader in the energy, transport, and technology sectors.

I am deeply grateful to my supervisors, Mateo Gomez, and Francois Zirikana for their unwavering support and the learning opportunities they have provided thus far.
