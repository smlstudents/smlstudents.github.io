Chryssie Karera: My internship experience at VWSR

July 25, 2024

My name is Chryssie Karera, a graduate student at CMU Africa, currently interning at Volkswagen Mobility Solutions Rwanda as part of the Carnegie Mellon University Africa summer internship program. I would like to share insights and updates on how my internship has gone so far:

Projects and Tasks: As a data analyst at VWSR, my main job is to work with data. My initial tasks involved collecting data from different VWSR departments and transforming it into visual representations to help the company understand its performance more easily. This was challenging because of the large volume of data, and I had to identify the most meaningful indicators. This required working closely with representatives from various departments to find the best metrics. One significant analysis I conducted was identifying hotspot areas for ride-hailing services, breaking it down by weekdays, hourly demand, and monthly trip numbers and averages. Additionally, I collaborated with departments that have daily operations to automate their data collection processes, which are now successfully in use. This task was complex as it involved understanding their requirements thoroughly and ensuring that key performance indicators could be easily measured with the new system.

Skills and Technologies: Throughout my work, I had to find the best technologies to accomplish my tasks. My skills in exploratory data analysis have significantly improved, especially as I worked with various departments to meet their needs.

Challenges: Every job comes with its challenges, and mine was no different. One of the main challenges was aggregating data from different sources and finding a representation solution that worked for all. Through persistence and experimentation, I eventually found a solution that was well-received by the VWSR team.

Memorable Experience: A memorable experience was presenting my work to the senior management at VWSR after two months. The presentation was well-received and is now guiding the team in implementing visual analytics solutions. Seeing the data collection solution I developed being used in their daily operations boosted my confidence and demonstrated the impact of my internship.

Career Goals: This internship has been crucial in shaping my career goals. It provided me with practical experience in applying data analysis skills learned at CMU and gave me exposure to working with a leading organization in the transport sector, both in Rwanda and worldwide. Although I never thought I would work in the transport sector, this experience has been incredibly motivating and has inspired me to pursue a career in data analysis, aiming to contribute to decision-making and the advancement of solutions using data as the main focus.
