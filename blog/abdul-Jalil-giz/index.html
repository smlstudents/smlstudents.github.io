Abdul Jalil Zakaria at GIZ Rwanda

July 25, 2024

Abdul is an alumnus of the CMU-Africa Class of 2024 within the MS-ECE Program. He works as a consultant with the Climate, Energy, and Sustainable Urban Development Cluster of GIZ Rwanda, contributing to various electric mobility project components. These projects aim to support the Rwandan government in electrifying public transport fleets in secondary/satellite cities and intercity transport, as well as promoting the electrification of the transport sector in Rwanda through capacity building and awareness raising.

His current responsibilities include supporting the development of a policy paper on minibus electrification, conducting research on capacity needs assessment for public transport electrification, and developing a concept for EV maintenance training for the private sector.

Abdul is thrilled to be part of the GIZ Mobility Team, working towards making mobility more sustainable and contributing to the transformation of the transport sector in Rwanda.
