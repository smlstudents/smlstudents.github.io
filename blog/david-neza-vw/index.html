I am excited to share my progress as an intern at the Smart Mobility Lab and Volkswagen Mobility Solutions Rwanda. I have had a great experience with Serge and his team, who have been instrumental in helping me achieve my goals and succeed in my work. Their feedback and support have been invaluable.

I am currently working on a driver profiling platform/system, which will be crucial in understanding the different metrics contributing to a driver’s efficiency and success. This platform will help Volkswagen Mobility Solutions Rwanda’s ride-hailing service “Move” continue to provide world-class services to its customers. By leveraging data science and technology, we can significantly improve the quality of services we offer.
