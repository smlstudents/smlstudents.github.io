About

Welcome to the Smart Mobility Lab Blog! This is a space where we share the exciting work and experiences of CMU-Africa students and graduates working with our partners like GIZ, Volkswagen, and Kabisa. Here, you'll find regular updates that highlight the valuable contributions of our interns and employees as they tackle real-world challenges in the smart mobility sector. Through these stories, we give you a glimpse into the projects and lessons that are shaping the future of mobility.
